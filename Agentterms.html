Terms and Conditions for Referral Agents

Important Guidelines

  • The term **"referral"** specifically means that the patient must undergo **treatment or surgery as an inpatient** in a hospital until their wellness and discharge.
  • **Referral commission** is provided only after the patient's **official discharge** from the hospital.
  • **Payment payouts** commence during the **first week of every month**.
  • Agents must understand that commissions are based **solely on the surgery cost**, and **not** on non-medical items such as room rent, food, or other related bills.
  • We do not support seeking **multiple hospitals for cost estimations** for a single patient. If a specific surgery is not feasible at one facility, we will then explore the next suitable option.
  • **Cost comparison is strictly prohibited**.
  • For any queries regarding **payments, appointments, flight bookings, or travel**, you must **inform us directly**. Do not provide any false assurances to patients without our prior knowledge or approval.
  • **Costs, facilities, and duration of stay** vary significantly from one location to another. Always **seek our suggestions** before making any assurances to patients.
  • If you are provided with images, and require further clarification or additional details, please **promptly send us your request** with proper communication and referral details.
